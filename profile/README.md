# 🎓 Office of Career Services, IIT Hyderabad

Welcome to the official GitHub organization of the **Office of Career Services (OCS)** at the **Indian Institute of Technology Hyderabad (IITH)**.

The OCS is the institute’s dedicated **placement and internship cell**, serving as a vital link between the student community and the professional world. This GitHub organization is maintained by the ***OCS Tech Cell*** -- a team of student developers working to streamline and automate OCS processes.

---

## 📁 Repositories

All repositories under this organization are **private** for obvious reason.

---

## 🤝 For Recruiters

We invite organizations to engage with IITH’s diverse and talented student pool.

- Internship details
- Past recruiters & statistics
